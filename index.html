7
Local jurisdiction — close residual gaps NIST and ISO cannot cover
Quebec Law 25 · LRSSS · local context ▼
ISO 27001 + 800-53 + Quebec Law 25 + LRSSS (Loi 5) → Best control
External influence — local jurisdiction gaps not covered by NIST/ISO
Quebec Law 25: privacy officer required · breach notification within 72hrs · PIA mandatory for high-risk processing
Law 25: explicit consent required for behavioral monitoring — any external influence detection program requires PIA
LRSSS (Loi 5, in force July 2024): health orgs must report anticipated confidentiality incidents — proactive obligation beyond NIST/ISO
LRSSS: annual access audit + mandatory staff training on health information protection (intersects A.6.3 and PM-12)
Local gap: no federal framework mandates a workforce cultural integrity monitoring program — Law 25 + A.5.7 hybrid requirement
Recommended: internal PIR program covering behavioral indicators, mapped to Law 25 breach conditions and LRSSS proactive reporting obligations
